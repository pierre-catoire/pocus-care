# POCUS-CARE

**Point-of-Care Ultrasound for Conflict-Affected Regions**

POCUS-CARE is an open-access educational initiative dedicated to improving the use of point-of-care ultrasound (POCUS) in humanitarian and resource-limited settings.

The project aims to provide high-quality, freely accessible educational resources for healthcare professionals working in emergency and acute care, with a particular focus on conflict-affected regions.

---

## Objectives

The project has six main objectives:

- Develop an evidence-based POCUS curriculum for emergency care.
- Produce freely accessible educational resources.
- Support training programmes in humanitarian settings.
- Promote local instructor development through a train-the-trainer approach.
- Foster collaboration between humanitarian organisations, universities, and professional societies.
- Encourage research and innovation in POCUS education.
